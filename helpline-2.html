How do I Add Resources?

Resources allow Admins and users to post resources, in the form of files, on the platform for others to see and use. The resources are displayed within folders on the Resources page. It is important to note that all files must be added to a folder and cannot stand alone.

Adding a New Resource Folder

Users who have the relevant permissions to post content on the resources page will be able to add new resource folders. Once they click the New Folder button on the top right, they will be taken to the “new folder” screen. Here they will be prompted to add.
